## Shiju Varghese: Spiritual Teacher 

Shiju Varghese is a practitioner and teacher of a non-dual spiritual path whose evolutionary journey is rooted in Satipaṭṭhāna Vipassanā, A Course in Miracles (ACIM), and Dzogchen. His spiritual path began in the early 2010s with Satipaṭṭhāna Vipassanā meditation, grounded in the teachings of Venerable Mahasi Sayadaw in the Theravada Buddhist tradition. In 2024, he began studying A Course in Miracles as a non-dual teaching. ACIM motivated him to explore a non-dual meditative path that led him to embody the path of Dzogchen in the Nyingma tradition of Tibetan Buddhism in 2026. He provides spiritual mentoring on Non-Duality, A Course in Miracles, and Satipaṭṭhāna Vipassanā meditation.
Shiju also provides psychotherapy by synthesizing trauma healing therapies—such as Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT) and the science of the autonomic nervous system (ANS)—with the spiritual wisdom of non-dual esoteric teachings.
