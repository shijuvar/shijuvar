## Shiju Varghese: Spiritual Teacher 

Shiju Varghese is a spiritual teacher who embodies a path rooted in A Course in Miracles (ACIM), Non-Duality, and the practice of Satipatthāna Vipassanā Meditation within the Theravada Buddhist tradition. He offers spiritual psychotherapy by synthesizing non-dual esoteric wisdom with the science of the autonomic nervous system and trauma-informed modalities—specifically integrating Polyvagal Theory and Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT). Shiju provides spiritual mentoring in both A Course in Miracles and Satipatthāna Vipassanā Meditation.
